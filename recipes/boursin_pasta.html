Creamy Boursin Pasta

Boursin pasta is a creamy, indulgent dish that combines the rich flavors of garlic and herb-infused Boursin cheese with pasta. This quick and easy recipe offers a velvety sauce that coats every piece of pasta, making it a perfect comfort meal.

The addition of fresh herbs, a hint of lemon, and optional vegetables like spinach or tomatoes brings extra freshness and brightness to the dish. Whether you're looking for a satisfying weeknight meal or something special for guests, Boursin pasta delivers flavor with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta (e.g., penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 1 package (150g) of Boursin Garlic & Herb cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 100ml heavy cream or milk (optional for extra creaminess)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh spinach (optional)
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Prepare the sauce: In a large pan, heat olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add Boursin cheese: Lower the heat and add the Boursin cheese to the pan, stirring until it begins to melt into a creamy sauce. If you'd like the sauce to be creamier, stir in the heavy cream or milk at this point.
  4. Combine with pasta: Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat in the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, gradually add some of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up until you reach your desired consistency.
  5. Add extras (optional): For added freshness, toss in a handful of fresh spinach and halved cherry tomatoes. Allow the spinach to wilt slightly and the tomatoes to soften.
  6. Finish with lemon and Parmesan: Stir in the lemon juice and zest, followed by the grated Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish and serve: Plate the pasta and garnish with fresh basil or parsley, along with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
