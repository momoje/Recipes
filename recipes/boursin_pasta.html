Creamy Boursin Pasta

Boursin pasta is a creamy, indulgent dish that combines the rich flavors of garlic and herb-infused Boursin cheese with pasta. This quick and easy recipe offers a velvety sauce that coats every piece of pasta, making it a perfect comfort meal.

The addition of fresh herbs, a hint of lemon, and optional vegetables like spinach or tomatoes brings extra freshness and brightness to the dish. Whether you're looking for a satisfying weeknight meal or something special for guests, Boursin pasta delivers flavor with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta (e.g., penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 1 package (150g) of Boursin Garlic & Herb cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 100ml heavy cream or milk (optional for extra creaminess)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh spinach (optional)
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
